Roohafza

Description

Rooh Afza (Soul Refresher) is a concentrated squash. It was formulated in 1906 in Ghaziabad, British India by Hakim Hafiz Abdul Majeed.

Ingredients

  • 1 Glass Water
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • Roohafza 2 spoons

Steps

  1. Put Sugar in water and mix it
  2. Add roohafza and mix properly
